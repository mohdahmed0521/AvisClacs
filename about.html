About Us

Avis Vascular Centre is India’s first and only Vascular and Interventional Radiology hospital in private practice. It was established in Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad, in 2015 by Dr. Rajah V Koppala after completing fellowships in Vascular and Interventional Radiology in the UK and USA.

Within months, the hospital quickly garnered a reputation for being the best hospital for varicose veins relief by offering the non-surgical laser treatment for varicose veins. Thousands of patients with swelling and ulcers returned home free from pain and symptoms.

By 2016, Dr. Koppala established two more branches in Bangalore and Vijayawada. As of 2024, Avis Vascular Centre has over 20 branches across India.

Benefits of Endovenous Laser Ablation Therapy

The highly qualified interventional radiologists at Avis Vascular Centre have provided Endovenous Laser Ablation Therapy (laser treatment) for varicose veins to more than 20,000 patients in the last decade.

CLaCS Treatment

In 2024, our founder, Dr. Rajah V Koppala, travelled to Brazil and imported the necessary equipment for the CLaCS treatment to India. With more than 20 years of experience treating chronic venous insufficiency worldwide, one would be hard-pressed to find an Indian doctor more qualified than Dr. Koppala to provide this treatment.
